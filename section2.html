Advanced Metrics

Modern analysts use metrics like PER, TS%, Offensive Rating, and Win Shares to measure a player’s impact beyond the box score. These metrics combine data to reveal efficiency and contribution per possession.

Summary of Key Advanced Metrics
Metric Full Name Measures What It Reveals
PER Player Efficiency Rating Overall performance per minute Summarizes a player’s contributions in one number
TS% True Shooting Percentage Shooting efficiency including 2PT, 3PT, and FT Shows how efficiently a player scores
OffRtg Offensive Rating Points produced per 100 possessions Measures scoring efficiency in team context
WS Win Shares Estimated wins contributed by a player Shows total impact on team success
