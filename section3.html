The Future of Basketball Analytics

The future of basketball analytics uses things like AI, machine learning, and wearable tech. Teams can now:

  • Track injury risks
  • Adjust training schedules
  • Look at live game data right on the sidelines

Coaches can use data to choose the best lineups, and players can use sensors to see when they’re getting too tired. Analytics isn’t just a small part of the game anymore, it’s a big part of how basketball works today.
